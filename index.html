Rodrickus Austin

Registered Nurse

Rodrickus has been working in the medical field for over 12 years. He worked as a certified pharmacy technician for six years. During that time, he also worked as a certified nursing assistant. He graduated nursing school in 2012 with an ADN from Jefferson State Community College in Birmingham Alabama. Rodrickus then went on to work at the Birmingham VA serving our veterans. He believes that extra care is needed when dealing with a vulnerable population such as our military veterans. He later when on to pursue his passion as a travel nurse in which he traveled to Lousiana, Missouri, and California. In 2015 he received his BSN from Jacksonville State University. Currently, he maintains Prn nursing positions in both California and Alabama. Rodrickus holds BLS and ACLS instructor certifications. He is a member of the American Nurses Association, Alabama State Nurses Association, National Nurses in Business Association, and Black Nurses Rock.
